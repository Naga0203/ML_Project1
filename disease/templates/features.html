Heart Disease Features Explanation

Age

Definition: Patient's age in years.

Example: 45, 60, 32

Sex

Definition: Biological sex of the patient.

Values: 0 = Female, 1 = Male

Chest Pain Type

Definition: Type of chest pain experienced by the patient.

Values:

  • 1 = Typical angina
  • 2 = Atypical angina
  • 3 = Non-anginal pain
  • 4 = Asymptomatic

Resting Blood Pressure

Definition: Blood pressure measured while resting (in mmHg).

Example: 120, 140, 160

Cholesterol

Definition: Serum cholesterol level (mg/dL).

Example: 200, 250, 300

Fasting Blood Sugar

Definition: Blood sugar level after fasting.

Values: 0 = < 120 mg/dL, 1 = â‰¥ 120 mg/dL

Resting ECG

Definition: Electrocardiographic results at rest.

Values:

  • 0 = Normal
  • 1 = ST-T wave abnormality
  • 2 = Left ventricular hypertrophy

Max Heart Rate

Definition: Maximum heart rate achieved during exercise.

Example: 150, 180, 200

ST Depression

Definition: Depression of the ST segment in ECG.

Example: 1.2, 2.5, 3.0

ST Slope

Definition: Slope of the ST segment during peak exercise.

Values:

  • 1 = Upsloping
  • 2 = Flat
  • 3 = Downsloping

Number of Major Vessels

Definition: Number of major blood vessels colored by fluoroscopy.

Values: 0 to 4

Thal

Definition: Thalassemia disease.

Values: 0 = No, 1 = Yes

Smoking and Alcohol

Definition: Smoking and alcohol consumption levels.

Values:

  • 0 = Non-smoker, Non-drinker
  • 1 = Smoker, Non-drinker
  • 2 = Non-smoker, Drinker
  • 3 = Smoker, Drinker

Exercise Angina and Physical Activity

Definition: Exercise-induced angina and physical activity levels.

Values:

  • 1 = No angina, Low physical activity
  • 2 = No angina, High physical activity
  • 3 = Angina present, Low physical activity
  • 4 = Angina present, High physical activity
